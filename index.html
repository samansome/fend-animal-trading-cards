Clownfish

Anemonefish are native to colder waters of the Indian and Percific oceans,including the Great Barrier Reef and the Red Sea.

  • Kingdom : Animalia
  • Phylum : Chordata
  • Class : Actinopterygii
  • Order : Perciformes.

Clownfish or anemonefish are omnivorous and can feed from undigested food from their host anemones,and the fecal matter from the anemonefish provides nutrients to the sea anemone.
